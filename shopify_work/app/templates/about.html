Our Mission

With the uprising growth of e-commerce, people are starting e-commerce businesses more than ever. We aim to build proper solutions to enable everyone to capitalize on the opportunity.

Why We Built SpySales

Founded in 2020 by brothers Nawras Ganim and Josef Ganim, they recognized the struggle store owners have with finding profitable products to sell online and are currently seeking to improve that process.

Long Term Vision

Develop more complex solutions that will detect new and upcoming products from more platforms and channels, aiming to become the go-to platform for e-commerce store owners seeking to expand their product catalogs.
